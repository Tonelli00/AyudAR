# AyudAR - Application to promote social collaboration
**AyudAR** is a web platform aimed at connecting people, non-governmental organizations (NGOs), and companies with the goal of facilitating collaboration in social causes.

The application allows those who need help to find people willing to collaborate, whether through:
- Financial donations.
- Volunteering.
- Professional contributions.
- Cause promotion.

The idea arises from a common problem: many people have the intention of helping, but they cannot easily find reliable organizations, causes aligned with their interests, or ways to collaborate according to their skills and availability.

Currently, a large part of charitable causes are shared through traditional social networks, where posts have a very short lifespan and often get lost among a large amount of content. This makes it difficult for people to discover collaboration opportunities and for organizations to reach potential volunteers or donors.

AyudAR seeks to centralize these causes in a platform exclusively dedicated to social impact, allowing organizations to showcase their needs and enabling users to easily find concrete ways to contribute.

---

# Main Objective
The main objective of **AyudAR** is to create a digital space that promotes collaboration and social growth, connecting people, organizations, and communities to facilitate solidarity actions and generate a positive impact on society.

# Main functionalities
## Registration/Creation of new users
- Registration and login
- Creation of personal profiles
- History of donations made

## Organizations / Users
- Profile for NGOs
- Creation of applications
- Volunteer search (NGOs)
- Publications for charitable causes
- Real-time control of funds raised or volunteers registered

## Volunteering
- Applications to proposals / Projects

## Communication
- Messaging system for communication between users
